Account Help

Common errors:
"You cannot delete the current user!" - This means that you are trying to delete the account you are currently signed into. To delete this account, sign into another administrator account and attempt to delete the account.
"No account selected!" - you are trying to perform an action (such as deletion) and there is no account selected. To select, simply click a name and it will become highlighted in blue.

Functions:
"Load Accounts" - will load all the currently registered user accounts on the system into the viewfinder.
"Add accounts" - will redirect you to the "Create Account" tab, where you can register a new user.
"Delete" - will attempt to delete the currently selected account. Press 'Yes' to confirm and 'No' to abort. 
"Save" - will save any and all changes made to the accounts registered in the system. THERE IS NO UNDO AFTER THIS POINT!
